Food

There is no sitdown meal however we do have a brazilian BBQ with a selection of seasonal salads. We also have pizza for the kids. We will also be putting out some seafood and local oysters. Menu  

Travel Arrangements

There are planned rail works on both the line from Victoria and Kings Cross, they have got rail replacement buses running though but it won't be super quick. There are 3 alternatives:
  • 1. You stay the whole weekend or at least one of the nights - A lot of the accomodation is booked up but there is still space in the premier inn
  • 2. You get the fast train to Ashford (35mins from London) and then get a 25min cab over to Faversham. Please be warned that getting a cab back will need to be arranged as the availability of cabs in the town is limited
  • 3. Go into Ashford (35mins from London) and catch the bus from Ashford to Faversham which takes around 50mins
 If you are staying in central faversham then it is about a 20-25min walk to the pub where the party is. We are endevouring to get a taxi shuttle service from the venue back into town to where you are staying.

What to wear?

This is a seriously low key event so come wearing what makes you feel fabulous. We are having an adult bouncy castle so if you want to bounce make sure you have some appropriate clothing
